About Us

We opened in October 2022 and serve up original Portuguese burgers, tasty kebabs, and crispy fish and chips – all freshly made just for you!

Our customers absolutely love our food! They say our burgers are the best, our kebabs are awesome, and our fish and chips are exceptional.

Come visit us and enjoy delicious meals while gazing at the beautiful waterfront. We're passionate about making yummy food just for you. See you soon at Harrington Breeze for a delightful dining experience!
